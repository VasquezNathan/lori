ABOUT ME

Massage isn't just a luxury. On a regular basis, therapeutic massage can help to improve health, rejuvenation, mood, and wellness. My goal is to intuitively listen to the body for a direction in treatment.

I began my journey in massage therapy in Jan. 2003. In 2007 I became a Holistic Health Practitioner (HHP), with over 1000 course hours in the theory, practice, and clinical application of massage therapy training. I have also completed 48 hours of continuing education in advanced anatomy and human dissection as well as my California Massage Therapy Council (CMATC) certification.
